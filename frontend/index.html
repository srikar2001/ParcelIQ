FAQ
Common questions
What states do you cover?+
Florida statewide during beta. All 67 counties. Other states coming soon.
Where does the data come from?+
Live government sources — FEMA flood maps, USFWS wetlands and wildlife, EPA contamination records, OpenStreetMap roads, Florida DOR parcel records. Every flag is cited with its source.
How accurate is it?+
We use the same data sources county GIS offices use. We cite every source so you can verify any flag independently.
Is this a substitute for due diligence?+
No. ParcelIQ eliminates the obvious kills fast. Always do a title search, survey, and manual utility check before closing.
What is the free trial?+
1,000 parcels per week free. No credit card required.
Can I share results with my team?+
Yes. Every batch has a shareable public link that works without logging in.
